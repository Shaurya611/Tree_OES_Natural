At OE Trees, we provide tree care across North, West and Mid Norfolk. We believe in engaging with clients to achieve the best results in terms of tree health and, where required, ensure that trees are felled or dismantled safely and in accordance with the highest standards – complying with the British Standards for tree work (BS3998).

We also discuss appropriate tree care with the local planning authority in relation to TPOs and Conservation Areas, so that all the necessary permissions are obtained on your behalf before any work is undertaken, wherever required.

Our services include:

  • Tree felling
  • Tree pruning
  • Tree planting
  • Land clearance
  • Green vegetation management
  • Hedge cutting
  • Stump grinding & removal
